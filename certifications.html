30 Days Yoga Volunteer

Purpose of the Program:
To prepare passionate individuals to become yoga volunteers who can:
• Assist in yoga sessions
• Support the spread of yogic values in their families, schools, or local communities
• Create awareness about physical, mental & emotional well-being through yoga
• Lay the foundation for a future teaching journey (optional)
⸻
Who Is This For?
• Students, homemakers, professionals & wellness lovers
• Anyone interested in seva (selfless service) through yoga
• Beginners who want to understand the role of yoga in community well-being
• Aspiring yoga teachers looking to begin with volunteering
⸻
Course Duration & Mode:
• Duration: 30 Days
• Format: Online (Live on Zoom)
• Timing: 60 mins/day (Weekend or Daily Batch Options)
• Language: Hindi & English
• Certification: Certified Yoga Volunteer from Yog Nishtha Trust
⸻
Course Structure:
Week 1: Introduction & Self-Practice
• What is a Yoga Volunteer? Roles & Responsibilities
• Yoga Philosophy: Yamas & Niyamas
• Daily Asana, Pranayama & Meditation
• Basic human values in yogic life
Week 2: Understanding Yoga for All
• Yoga for different age groups (kids, elderly, women)
• How to create awareness about yoga in your circle
• Basic health benefits of yoga – mind, body & soul
• 15-min daily practice module
Week 3: Community Involvement & Assistance Skills
• Assisting your trainer during yoga classes
• Conducting simple warm-ups or ending meditations
• Helping others overcome hesitation or fear of yoga
• How to inspire non-practitioners with your journey
Week 4: Outreach & Volunteer Project
• Design your Yoga Seva project: School, family, group, or online
• Create a 5-day awareness plan with our support
• Present your volunteer report to complete certification
• Final Q&A, sharing & closing ceremony
⸻
What You Get:
• Daily LIVE classes +PPT
• Certification as a Yoga Volunteer
• One 1-on-1 session with Nishtha for guidance
• Lifetime access to Yog Nishtha community group
• Daily Task to Perform
