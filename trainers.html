Founder & Head Instructor

Nishtha Chaudhary

MA in Yoga and Naturopathy from UTTRAKHAND University 200 Hours Internationaly Certified YTT @Yogaalliance

Nishtha leads the training team with expertise and passion, ensuring every client reaches their highest potential. Founded and guided by flexibility expert Nishtha Chaudhary, our mission is to help people of all ages discover the transformative power of yoga through a modern, yet deeply rooted yogic lifestyle. With years of experience in guiding students towards improved flexibility, strength, and inner harmony, Nishtha combines classical asanas with a mindful approach to movement and breath. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced practitioner, our hybrid classes (online + offline) are thoughtfully designed to suit every individual’s needs.

Science Educator & Human Anatomy Expert

Richa Chaudhary

M.Sc. in Zoology

Richa Chaudhary is a passionate science teacher and a subject matter expert in human anatomy. With a strong academic background and years of teaching experience, she brings a clear and insightful understanding of the human body to the Yog Nishtha Trust community. Her ability to connect scientific principles with yogic practices helps deepen the learning experience for students and practitioners alike. Richa’s engaging teaching style and deep knowledge make her an invaluable part of the trust’s educational and wellness initiatives.

Meditation & Wellness Teacher

Mayuri

200 Hours YTT from Patanjali Yog Peeth South India

With a calm presence and a compassionate heart, Mayuri brings a deep sense of peace and clarity to everyone she teaches. As a dedicated meditation and wellness trainer, she guides individuals on the path of inner stillness, emotional balance, and holistic well-being. Whether you’re just beginning your meditation journey or seeking to deepen your practice, Mayuri’s approach is nurturing, intuitive, and rooted in authentic yogic wisdom. At Yog Nishtha Trust, Mayuri plays a key role in helping students reconnect with their inner selves—creating space for healing, awareness, and lasting transformation.
