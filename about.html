Founder

Nishtha Chaudhary

Nishtha Chaudhary , The visionary behind Yog Nishtha Trust, is a dedicated yoga practitioner and wellness advocate with a deep-rooted passion for promoting holistic living. With years of personal practice and teaching experience, Nishtha founded Yog Nishtha Trust to make authentic yogic practices accessible to all—regardless of age, background, or lifestyle. Driven by a commitment to well-being, Nishtha integrates traditional yoga philosophies with modern needs, offering a nurturing space for physical health, mental clarity, and spiritual balance. Under her guidance, Yog Nishtha has become a trusted name in hybrid (online + offline) yoga classes that foster both personal transformation and community connection. Nishtha believes in the power of discipline (Nishtha) and intuition as key forces in the journey of self-discovery, and she continues to inspire others to lead balanced, mindful lives through yoga.
